Why we built this

Connection is the cure.

Loneliness is the defining health crisis of our time. Running alone fixes your body. Running together fixes your mind.

We built Runklub because the hardest part of starting — or staying consistent — isn't fitness. It's finding people. The right people. At the right time. Near you.

Every run club you join is a community that shows up for you. A reason to get out of bed at 5:30am. A group that notices when you're not there. That's what we're building — not just an app, but infrastructure for human connection through sport.

"The best technology disappears into the background and leaves only the human moment. We want Runklub to be the thing you never notice — and the community you never forget."
🧠
Mental wellbeing first. Group exercise reduces cortisol and builds the social bonds that buffer stress and depression.
💪
Physical accountability. Community is the most powerful training tool ever built. We're just the map.
🌍
Built to travel. Whether you're local or backpacking through Europe — your Runklub community moves with you.
🔗
Network as moat. The community we build together is something no algorithm can replicate.
How it works

Simple.
By design.

01

Drop your pin

Open Runklub, share your location, and instantly see every active club and group run near you — sorted by distance, pace, and schedule.

02

Find your pace

Filter by distance, speed, vibe, or time of day. Beginner-friendly 5km socials. Fast track sessions. Trail crews. There's a klub for every runner.

03

Spin up a klub

No club near you? Start one in under 60 seconds. Name it, set your pace, pick your days. Runklub notifies nearby runners who match your vibe.

04

Run. Repeat.

RSVP, show up, connect. Your community grows with every session. The more you run together, the stronger the network becomes for everyone.
