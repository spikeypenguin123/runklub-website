08 · Principles

Rules to break, eventually.

Five rules to keep the system honest as it scales across the app, website, Instagram, and everything downstream.

01

Colour has meaning.

Coral = action. Sage = community. Gold = accent. Ink = structure. If a colour isn't doing its job, don't use it.

02

The gradient is a moment.

Max three gradient surfaces per screen. Reserve it for the logo, splash, hero CTAs, and celebrations.

03

Canvas, not white.

Every screen sits on the warm off-white. Pre-dawn eyes first. Never default to pure white or grey.

04

One font, many weights.

DM Sans, everywhere. Regular for body, bold for UI, 900 italic for wordmarks. No second family, ever.

05

Maps are the art.

No stock photography. The map, the pin, the place — those are the visuals. Keep the UI clean so location can shine.
